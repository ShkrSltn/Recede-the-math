Primary School Math Cheatsheet

Basic Math Operations

  • Addition (+): Adding two numbers: 3 + 4 = 7
  • Subtraction (−): Subtracting one number from another: 9 - 5 = 4
  • Multiplication (×): Multiplying two numbers: 6 × 3 = 18
  • Division (÷): Dividing one number by another: 12 ÷ 4 = 3

Number Properties

  • Even Numbers: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, ...
  • Odd Numbers: 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, ...
  • Prime Numbers: Numbers greater than 1 with no divisors other than 1 and themselves: 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, ...
  • Composite Numbers: Numbers greater than 1 that have divisors other than 1 and themselves: 4, 6, 8, 9, 10, ...

Place Value

  • Units, Tens, Hundreds, Thousands:
  • Example: In the number 3,482:
    • 2 is in the Units place
    • 8 is in the Tens place
    • 4 is in the Hundreds place
    • 3 is in the Thousands place

Fractions

  • Basic Terms:
    • Numerator: The top part of a fraction
    • Denominator: The bottom part of a fraction
  • Types of Fractions:
    • Proper Fraction: Numerator < Denominator (e.g., 3/4)
    • Improper Fraction: Numerator > Denominator (e.g., 5/3)
    • Mixed Number: A whole number and a fraction (e.g., 2 1/2)
  • Converting Improper Fractions to Mixed Numbers:

    Example: 7/3 = 2 1/3

Decimals

  • Place Value:
    • Tenths, Hundredths, Thousandths
    • Example: In 3.456:
      • 4 is in the Tenths place
      • 5 is in the Hundredths place
      • 6 is in the Thousandths place
  • Converting Fractions to Decimals:
    • 1/2 = 0.5
    • 3/4 = 0.75
